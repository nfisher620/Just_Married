About Us

Just Married "popped the question" to founder Nicole Fisher after she got married to her husband Ben on June 20th, 2015 in the beautiful city of San Clemente.


After 9 months of planning for the big day, she found herself wanting to know where other brides got their vendors, ideas, etc. She wanted to create a one-stop shop for brides to plan their dream wedding and hone in their inner David Tutera.
